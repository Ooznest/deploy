1) Connect USB-C Cable

Connect USB-C Cable to your computer. Once connected, the next step is to choose one bootloader path below, depending on the state of the controller firmware.

Next: Choose a bootloader method below

2) Manual Bootloader Entry

Use this for controllers with no firmware or corrupted firmware.

• Press and hold the MODE button,

• then click RESET, then release MODE.

• Click Connect Device.

2) Automatic Bootloader Entry

Use this for updating controllers already running grblHAL v20260622 or newer.

• Click Enter Bootloader.


This will trigger a $BOOTLOADER command on the controller, then prompt you to re-connect to the Flashing port

3) Flash grblHAL

Once controller had entered bootloader, click Flash grblHAL to deploy the firmware.
